About BGMI

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is a popular free-to-play battle royale game for mobile devices. Developed by KRAFTON, it brings a familiar and thrilling gameplay experience to Indian players, with a focus on fair play and community engagement. Players parachute onto a deserted island, scavenge for weapons and gear, and fight to be the last person or team standing. The game features various maps, modes, and events, constantly evolving to keep the experience fresh and exciting.

BGMI is not just a game; it's a cultural phenomenon, with a massive esports scene and a dedicated community of content creators and players. Join millions of players and prove your skills in the ultimate battle for survival.

Royale Pass

The Royale Pass is a tiered reward system in BGMI. By completing daily and weekly missions, players can earn RP points and level up their pass. Each level unlocks new rewards, including exclusive outfits, weapon skins, emotes, and more.

There are two types of Royale Pass: the Free Pass and the Elite Pass. The Elite Pass offers even more rewards for players who purchase it with UC (in-game currency). Don't miss out on the chance to customize your character and stand out on the battlefield!

Esports

BGMI has a thriving and competitive esports ecosystem. Professional players and teams compete in major tournaments for huge prize pools, showcasing their skills and strategies to a global audience. The official BGMI esports circuit includes various stages, from open qualifiers to grand finals, providing a clear path for aspiring players to make a name for themselves.
